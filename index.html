You Grow, Girl!

You grow, girl!

In this game, you'll plant flowers and fight against weeds to grow them strong and beautiful. Each time you solve a number-based puzzle your flower will grow and the weeds will slowly die away. But too many wrong answers will cause your flowers to wither!

Before we get started, you'll need to pick a flower. Each flowers starts at a different level of health, making some more challenging to grow than others. Next, you'll choose a weed to fight against and what kind of math you'd like to practice.

First, choose which flower to grow:

  • Daisies are a great start for a garden. Pick this flower to ease into it.
  • Sunflowers are good at growing big and tall, this is good place to start.
  • Tulips can be tricky sometimes. Pick this flower for a bit of a challenge.

Next, choose which kind of weed to battle:

  • Level 1: Addition and subtraction
  • Level 2: Addition, subtraction, multiplication, and divsion
  • Level 3: Larger numbers with addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Select a flower and weed before you start!

Let's get growing! Your goal is to make the set of numbers beneath your flower equal the number to the right. You can do this using any combination of addition or subtraction . Each time you're correct, your flower's strength will increase and the weed's strength will decrease. But, if you're not correct or if you're not able to solve the puzzle within 60 seconds, the weed's strength will grow and your flower's strength decreses. Each time your flower's strength increases its ability to grow also increases and it will grow even more next time. Once the flower's strength reaches 100% it will added to your garden. Your garden can hold up to ten plants.

Grow your flower by solving the problem. Find the right order of addition, subtraction, multiplication, or division. Hit the grow button when you think you have it!
